Directions
  • From Rt. 3 North or South, take Exit 20.
  • Turn on to 3A North for approx 1.7 miles.
  • Turn Right onto Chestnut Street ( Just past the Bluerish River Tavern Restaurant).
  • Follow Chestnut Street for .7 miles. You will see a flagpole straight ahead.
  • At the flagpole rotary, take 2nd right onto Standish Street; Go between the Yellow building (on the right) and Duxbury Pizza (on the left).
  • Continue down Standish Street until the road turns sharply to the right.
  • Cedar Hill driveway is straight ahead marked by a sign to the right of the driveway.
