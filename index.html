Welcome to Cedar Hill
 

Cedar Hill is a non-denominational retreat center founded on and guided by Unitarian principles. We are stewards of an intimate retreat space in a unique, natural setting; a sanctuary for learning, growth and enrichment.   

Cedar Hill sits on 12 acres of conservation land with a picturesque path to 500 feet of private beach on the bay.

 

Cedar Hill can accommodate day groups up to 30 and can sleep up to 16 people.

 
We have a fully-equipped kitchen, large dining, living and family rooms, two full and two half baths. There are five bedrooms that sleep a total of 13 with an additional 3 trundle beds bringing the total up to 16, all beds are twin beds. One bedroom sleeps 1 (with one trundle bed); two bedrooms sleep 2; one sleeps 4; and one sleeps 4 with an additional two trundle beds.


Outside, we have a porch with rocking chairs and a vista of the cedar trees leading to the ocean, an outside shower, grill and fire pit.




 
 

The retreat center retains its heritage & charm and is a model for having a minimal carbon footprint and sustaining biodiversity in natural areas.

 
Our protected grounds, preserved and conserved in their natural and open space condition, offer guests a beautiful, natural setting for relaxation and gatherings on 12 acres of fields enhanced by wildflowers, stately cedars, and 500 feet of pristin, private beach.
 
 
 
Come Experience
