Cedar Hill Retreat Center Presents

We Are All Whalers With Dr. Michael Moore

Friday, November 8, 2024

7-9 PM

at First Parish Church Duxbury (Rt 3A)

 

Dr. Michael Moore is an accomplished speaker, author, and veterinary research scientist at Woods Hole Institute. He focuses on North Atlantic Right Whale survival and welfare and works with stakeholders to establish "buoyless" on-demand fishing systems to enable trap fishers and whales to coexist successfully.
