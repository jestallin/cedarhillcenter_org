About Us
 
 
Cedar Hill Retreat Center is owned and operated as a Massachusetts non-profit
corporation under the leadership of our Board of Directors.
 
We don't have an onsite office, so the best way to reach us is by e-mail
cedarhillretreat@gmail.com or by phone 781-217-4540.
We will be happy to provide information and answer all your questions.
 
Many diverse groups use our facilities for their classes, conferences and retreat needs:
parish staff, church groups, community groups, social service agencies, yoga groups,
youth groups, school groups and faculty, and small non-profit organizations.
 
Since your needs are unique, please e-mail us, and we will be glad to
gather the details of your stay and give you a quote based on your needs.
